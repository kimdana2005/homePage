We set ourselves the following goals

  • Develop and implement an ecological project.
  • Creating conditions for the formation of an active civic position, ecological culture and ecological awareness of students.
  • To contribute to moral, aesthetic and labor education.
  • Educate and develop a careful attitude of students to the world around them.
  • Involve the search for mechanisms for solving the local community's current problems through the development and implementation of socially significant projects.
  • Form a sense of personal responsibility for the state of the environment;
  • Creating conditions for the development of initiative and creativity through the organization of socially significant activities.
